Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2025

Home Keeper – Smart Inventory ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data through our mobile applications on Android and iOS.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address – for user authentication and inventory collaboration
  • Camera & Storage Access – for attaching photos to items and extracting text using ML Kit and OpenAI
  • Microphone Access
  • Usage Data – including crash logs and interaction data via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and enable app access
  • To store and manage inventory data in Firebase
  • To extract text from images using ML Kit and OpenAI
  • To analyze usage and crashes to improve the app
  • To provide smart inventory queries using OpenAI APIs

3. AI Credit Usage Tracking

If you use features powered by OpenAI (such as smart item suggestions or AI assistant queries), we may record limited data about your usage of these features, including:

  • Number of AI requests made
  • Total tokens consumed (prompt + completion)
  • AI model used (e.g., GPT-4, GPT-3.5)
  • Timestamp of your requests
  • Internal feature that triggered the request
  • Country where the request was made (determined by IP)
  • If country resolution fails, the anonymized IP address is temporarily stored

This information is used to monitor feature usage, manage free credit or subscription limits, detect abuse, and improve our services. None of the AI-generated responses or prompt content is stored in a way that links them to your identity. We do not share AI usage logs with third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): authentication, analytics, crash reporting, and data storage – Privacy Policy
  • ML Kit (by Google): We use Google's ML Kit on-device Text Recognition to extract text from images you provide while creating or editing inventory items. This text extraction happens entirely on your device, and your image is not transmitted to Google servers. However, ML Kit may send anonymized usage and performance metrics to Google to improve their services. Learn more at ML Kit Terms & Privacy.
  • OpenAI: We use OpenAI's API in two ways. First, we use it to power the AI assistant for smart inventory queries. Second, after extracting text from your image using ML Kit, we send that text along with the inventory field list to OpenAI’s API to generate intelligent suggestions for filling out item details. This means the extracted text is transmitted to OpenAI’s servers for processing. Review OpenAI's Usage Policies and Privacy Policy for more details.

5. Private Sharing and Collaborators

Users may choose to share their inventory data with specific individuals (such as family or friends) by inviting them via email. Shared access is strictly private and limited to explicitly invited collaborators. The app does not publicly display, broadcast, or host any user-generated content for public or community access.

7. Reporting Inappropriate Content

8. Account & Data Deletion

Users can delete their accounts from the app. This will delete:

  • Your profile
  • Your inventory and shared data
  • All related data stored in Firebase

Note: We do not control OpenAI’s internal data logging. See their policy for deletion rights.

Data Deletion

Users have full control over their data and can choose to delete either:

  • All items in their inventory without deleting their account:
    • Open the app
    • Go to Settings
    • Select Delete All Items in Inventory
  • Specific inventories they own:
    • Open the app
    • Go to Settings → Manage Inventories
    • Select the inventory and choose Delete Inventory (only if you are the owner)
  • Their entire account and all associated data:
    • Open the app
    • Go to Settings
    • Tap Delete Account under the Danger Zone section

Account deletion will permanently remove your user profile and all associated data from our servers (Firebase). For any additional help, users can contact us at bennyjon13@gmail.com.
